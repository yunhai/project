About Noodle88


WELCOME TO Noodle88

A small bunch of lads with common principle of providing high quality food & promoting healthy eating behaviour, yet maintaining the great taste of authentic curries, pooled together to serve you your everyday dinner. Our meal has no MSG, no artificial colouring or preservatives and we aim to use reduced salt, sugar and oil as much as possible.


Want to know how we do it..!!?

All our dishes are freshly prepared on site with fresh ingredients. Our vegetables and meats are sourced daily and locally by skilled buyers. Our key spice, Garam Masala, is roasted and grounded by ourselves to bring the aroma & taste to its best.
We are committed to bring food safety & food hygiene above the standard so that you can enjoy your takeaway meals as healthy & hygienic as at your home and as tasty and mouth-watering as in starred restaurant. Our head chefs have several years’ experience in art of cooking fine Indian menus in reputed restaurant. If you want to meet our chefs and discuss our recipes or inspect our kitchen, you are most welcome. We have open plan kitchen so you can see where and how your meals are prepared.
Beyond this, we love environment so we use environment friendly food packaging materials. Our chosen takeaway boxes not only provide you with steaming hot food but are also 100% recyclable.

Opening time : Monday-Thrusday 5:30 to 11:00, Friday - Saturday 5:00 to 11:00 & Sunday 5:30 to 10:30
